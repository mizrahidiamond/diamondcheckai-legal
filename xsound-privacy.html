XSound Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

XSound is a free app that controls Bose SoundTouch speakers over your local WiFi network. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data XSound does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

None. XSound does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data whatsoever.

How XSound Works

XSound communicates exclusively with:

  • Your Bose SoundTouch speaker — directly over your local WiFi network (never through the internet)
  • TuneIn Radio — to browse and search radio stations (no account or login required)

No Accounts

XSound does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information.

No Analytics or Tracking

XSound does not use any analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

No Third-Party Sharing

Since we collect no data, there is no data to share with third parties.

Local Storage

XSound stores your favorites and speaker connection history locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted anywhere and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Network Permissions

XSound requires local network access to discover and communicate with your Bose SoundTouch speaker. This permission is used solely for speaker control and is never used for any other purpose.

Children's Privacy

XSound does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at xsound@rafimizrahi.com.
